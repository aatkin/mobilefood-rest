Mobileservice/MobileREST: usage instructions

Restaurant GET-requests

The MobileREST-system currently supports only GET-requests. POST, PUT and DELETE are not yet supported (however they may be supported in the future).

Latest foodlist for a given :chain and :year may be retrieved as follows:

GET http://www.anssikin.eu/mobilerest/:chain/:year/current

Specific foodlist for a given :chain, :year and :week may be retrieved as follows:

GET http://www.anssikin.eu/mobilerest/:chain/:year/:week

Currently supported restaurant chains are Unica. Other restaurants chains are planned, such as Sodexo.

Miscellaneous

This information page can always be reached with the following request:

GET http://www.anssikin.eu/mobilerest/info

Parser logs for debugging can be viewed with the following request:

GET http://www.anssikin.eu/mobilerest/log
